how to meditate

  1. Sitting half-lotus is recommended but any comfortable position will do.
  2. close your eyes
  3. take 3 deep breaths
  4. breathe in, thinking of the word “ in” as you breathe in. Then, think “out” as you breathe out.
  5. continue breathing while labeling your breathes “in”, “out”, “in”, “out”.

via Thich Nhat Hanh & the Vipassana Dhura Society.
